About Carlos Sanchez

Originally from Cuba, Carlos Sanchez is a fine example of where ambition and drive can take you, despite where you come from. With nearly 40 years of experience in the industry, there isn’t a challenge within a loan that he can’t handle or overcome. Since moving to Los Angeles and starting Carlos Sanchez & Associates in 1986, he has been one of the top Loan Originators in the country winning numerous production awards and has maintained solid positive relationships with lenders and borrowers alike.

A trusted man by many, Carlos Sanchez always does what it takes to follow through in a professional fashion. Outside of the office, he’s a family man. He enjoys spending time with his daughter as well as traveling.
