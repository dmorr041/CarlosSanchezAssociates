Loan Programs

Being around for 30 years has helped us to create relationships with the best lenders and secure the lowest rates in the business. With our expertise and our qualified team working hard on every loan, you’re guaranteed to get the best service and best value for your purchase or refinance.

From the traditionally done Conventional loans to the higher amounted Jumbo loans, we’ve seen it all and can handle virtually any situation that crosses our desks. We also specialize in FHA and VA loans which not only service our men and women in service, but people with less down payment available to buy their dream home. We do it all.

Conventional Loans
  • Loans up to $679,000 (Larger amounts with multifamily)
  • As low as 5% down payment
  • Secondary Financing available
  • Interest only available
  • Adjustable rates available
  • Bank statement Loans available
FHA Loans
  • Loans up to $679,000
  • As low as 3.5% down payment
  • Credit Scores as low as 580 accepted
  • More forgiving debt to income ratios
  • Investor lending programs
VA Loans
  • Loans up to $679,000
  • Must be a Veteran
  • No down payment required in most cases
  • Lower average interest rates
  • No mortgage insurance premiums to pay
Jumbo/Private Loans
  • No loan limits!
  • Flexible Qualification
  • Details vary case by case
