Frequently Asked Questions

Funding your new home can be quite confusing, especially for first time buyers, so we've created a list of some frequently asked questions to better serve you, and clarify anything you may be curious about.

  • How quickly will I know if I’m approved or not?

    • It depends on how complicated the file is. Purchases take longer than refinances but both can be pre-qualified within a day.

  • I don’t have a lot of money for down payment. Is that ok?

    • It’s something we can work with. There are programs allowing for a down payment to be as little as 3.5%!

  • How long have you been in business?

    • 31 years and counting in the same office!

  • Are you licensed in other states?

    • Yes, we are licensed in both California and Florida.

  • How can I be sure that I’m getting the lowest rate?

    • All of our compensation plans are locked despite what rate we give you. So we always have the incentive to give you the very best rate that’s available!

  • What’s the best Loan program for me?

    • Give us a call and explain your situation. Tell us what you’re looking for and we’ll be happy to present all the options that make sense!
